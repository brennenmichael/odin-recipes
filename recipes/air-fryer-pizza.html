Air Fryer Pizza

This air fryer pizza is one of my favorites. Not only is it super easy and super quick to make, but it's a lot healthy than regular pizza (and a lot cheaper too!

Ingredients:

  • Naan bread (shaped like a circle)
  • Cheese of choice
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni, sausage, or any other toppings you see fit!

Steps:

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees. Set the air fryer timer to 8 minutes.
  2. While fryer is preheating, spread pizza sauce onto naan bread.
  3. Sprinkle cheese on top of sauce.
  4. Top with any toppings you see fit. Be careful with lighter toppings like pepperoni -- they may go flying! I find that either covering them with cheese or pressing them down into the sauce/bread helps.
  5. Load into basket or onto tray and let cook.
  6. Let cool and feel your connection to mother Italia.
