Air Fryer Garlic Parm Tater Tots

Simple. Savory. Satisfying. If you have no time and no shame (I personally have neither), then this is the recipe for you.

Ingredients:

  • Frozen tater tots
  • Garlic powder
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees. Set the air fryer timer to 8 minutes.
  2. Load tater tots into tray or basket and insert.
  3. Shake around halfway through.
  4. Once the tater tots are done cooking. generously sprinkle with garlic powder and parmesan cheese.
  5. Enjoy!
